Our Sevens Penalty Moves

Bomber 1

  • Key points are that player 1 triggers the play.
  • Players 5 & 6 are the main decoy pod so sell it!
  • Player 4 steps through the gap created by player 3 stepping to the side.
  • Players 2 & 3 turn to become immediate support!

Bomber 2

  • Key points are that player 1 triggers the play.
  • Player 4 is now the decoy so starts his run a fraction early.
  • Players 5 & 6 are the main pod and recieve the ball from player 1 as he loops around
  • Players 7 & 1 become immediate support!

Bomber 3

  • Key points are that player 1 triggers the play.
  • Players 4, 5 & 6 are now decoys.
  • Player 7 drifts wide/narrow depending on the defence tracking him, as the play is being setup!
  • Playerstill loops and takes the ball back from 2 before dropping a kick for Player 7.
